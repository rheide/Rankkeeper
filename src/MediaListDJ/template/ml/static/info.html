Welcome to RankKeeper!

RankKeeper is a service that you can use to keep track of everything you've rated on the internet: movies, tv shows, books, beer, restaurants, you name it. RankKeeper will present you with a list of all the things you've rated. It'll also give you the option of tracking your progress. Here's how it works:

  • Type the name of your movie/book/item and search on RankKeeper, OR:
  • Paste the link directly from a supported website
  • Rate it, set your progress if you like, and you're done!


RankKeeper can keep track of your ratings on other websites and synchronize your ratings. You can still continue to use your preferred website of choice while enjoying the benefits of RankKeeper's more advanced rating system.

All your ratings can be viewed easily in a list. Your list can be shared with others if you like. We respect your privacy, of course, so you decide if you want to publish your list or not. Click here to see an example of a public list.

RankKeeper is a young project and has only just started. There will be many new features in the future. Here's a sneak preview of some of the things we've got planned:

  • Import your list from other sites such as amazon.com, and many others.
  • Statistics on your progress: think of how thrilled you'll be after finding out that you've spent 6 months of your life watching tv shows!
  • Recommendations: find out similar things of any type: books, movies, anything!

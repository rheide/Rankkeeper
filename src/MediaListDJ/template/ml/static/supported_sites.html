IMDb

Pasting links

Using IMDb, you can rate movies, tv shows and tv show episodes. Rating an item is easy: just paste the url from IMDb into the textfield and press the Add button. Here's some examples:

  • http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0898266 - "The Big Bang Theory" - A TV Show
  • http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0209144 - "Memento" - A movie
  • http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1632224 - "The Robotic Manipulation" - An episode of a TV Show

Set up site sync to import your vote history automatically every night

IMDb keeps track of everything you've voted on, but it doesn't store the date of your vote, or when your vote changed. You can set up RankKeeper to import your vote history automatically every night, and it will automatically keep track of your ratings. You can continue to use IMDb to rate stuff and use RankKeeper to keep progress, or you can switch to RankKeeper altogether. It's your choice.

To set up the synchronization you must first mark your IMDb vote history as public. Use this page: http://www.imdb.com/mymovies/list?votehistory.

After you've made your vote history public, go to this page on RankKeeper to add your list to be imported. The link to your list page should look like this:

  • http://www.imdb.com/mymovies/list?l=01234567

RankKeeper will now update your ratings from IMDb every night. Every time you change a rating on IMDb, RankKeeper will add a new rating for you here. You can change your progress and status for the imported ratings on RankKeeper without the importer adding a new rating.

Technical note: the importer can perform its work a lot faster if you increase the number of titles on your vote history page.

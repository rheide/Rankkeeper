About RankKeeper

RankKeeper is a work-in-progress project that aims to provide the user the ability to rate any media that he has seen, read or experienced. This can be anything from movies and books to toys, rides in an amusement park or even a dish at a local restaurant.

RankKeeper aims to create a database of all kinds of media that will be maintained by a community of users. Its primary goal is to provide you with an all-in-one application that keeps track of everything you've seen and done, and what you thought of it. It's easy to forget that movie that you saw five years ago but can't remember the title of. With this website you won't ever forget again.

You can compare your ratings with that of others, and you can add users as friends to see what they've done recently, and what they thought of it. You'll be able to find people with similar taste in a variety of subjects.

The goal of this site is not to replace existing sites like IMDB or Amazon, but rather to augment them. Each individual site is usually aimed towards the media in question (IMDB for movies, Amazon for books, etc.), but none of them is geared towards the user. It's easy to see what all users thought of a particular movie or book, but it's not easy to see if a user who liked the movie also liked the book.

This website is still very much a work in progress and we are always looking for talented developers to help. If you have interest and experience in the following fields then please contact us.

  • Web development - user interface design and graphics design
  • Community management - forum administration, moderating etc.
  • Media - we need movie names, book names etc. to import into our database. Lists of media and import tools are required.
